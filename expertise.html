Seismic Analysis & Retrofit

We recognize that many structures require earthquake design to safeguard against failures, particularly in high-risk zones. Our engineers create solutions that not only resist seismic ground motions but are also economical, meeting Uniform Building Code (UBC) requirements.

Dynamic Analysis

We create a mathematical model to represent the physical structure, showing the spatial distribution of mass and stiffness to perform adequate calculations.

Equivalent Lateral Forces

A static force procedure to engineer a structure for ground motion, considering vertical and horizontal distribution, overturning, and drift limitation.

Modal Superposition

A simplified structural model with lumped mass, suitable for smaller bridges but may not yield enough information for more complex crossings.

Design Response Spectrum Analysis

An elastic dynamic analysis using the peak response of all significant modes to determine the structural response, based on site-specific characteristics.

Time-History Analysis

An analysis of the dynamic response of a structure when its base is subjected to a specific ground motion time history record.

Non-Linear Time-History Analysis

A detailed analysis of the lateral force system when a time history is not available, modeling the capacities and characteristics of nonlinear elements.
